## Problem

India's Zomato/Swiggy delivery partners lose 20–30% of monthly income
when rain, floods, or extreme heat forces them off the road —
with zero compensation and no safety net.

## Solution

GigShield tracks daily shift activity via GPS + AI, detects disruptions via
live weather and AQI APIs, and automatically credits ₹300 after 12 AM
to the worker's UPI account — no claim form, no waiting.
